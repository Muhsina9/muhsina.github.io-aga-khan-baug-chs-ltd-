Opening of the Aga Khan Baug, Versova

By His Late Highness Aga Khan IV
Mumbai, India · 17 January 1983

His Late Highness Aga Khan IV expressed profound pleasure to return to Versova to formally open the Aga Khan Baug housing project, conceived to restore dignity, stability, and hope to families facing urban poverty.

Declaring the housing ready to receive its first residents, he described the project as more than a construction milestone — it was a social foundation intended to uplift generations.

He emphasised housing as the first ingredient of self-respect, capable of transforming family life, work ethic, and future opportunity.
