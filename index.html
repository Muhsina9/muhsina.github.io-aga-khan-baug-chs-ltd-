About Aga Khan Baug

Established in 19XX

Aga Khan Baug Housing Society has been a peaceful residential community rooted in cooperation, mutual respect, and shared responsibility. Over the years, the society has grown into a close-knit neighborhood that values transparency, safety, and togetherness.

Managed by an elected committee and supported by its residents, the society continues to preserve its legacy while adapting thoughtfully to modern needs.
