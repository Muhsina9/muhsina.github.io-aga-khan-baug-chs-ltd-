About Aga Khan Baug Housing Society

Established in 19XX

Aga Khan Baug Housing Society is a close-knit residential community built on the principles of mutual respect, cooperation, and harmony. Over the decades, the society has grown into a peaceful neighborhood that values transparency in governance and safety for all residents.

Managed by an elected committee and supported by its members, the society continues to preserve its heritage while thoughtfully adapting to the needs of modern living.
